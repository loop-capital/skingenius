# Anti-Aging Peptides & Structural Ingredients — Knowledge Base

> Reference for ingredient mechanism of action and recommendation logic

## Category 1: Structural Remodelers (Matrixyls & Copper)

These ingredients act as cellular messengers, tricking the skin into believing it has been minorly injured so it drastically accelerates the production of structural proteins.

### Copper Tripeptide-1 (GHK-Cu)
A gold standard in tissue regeneration. Upregulates metalloproteinases (enzymes that clear out degraded, stiff collagen) and replaces them with fresh, springy Type I and Type III collagen. Accelerates epidermal stem cell proliferation, noticeably thickening thinning skin.

### Palmitoyl Tripeptide-1 & Palmitoyl Tetrapeptide-7 (Matrixyl 3000)
This duo forms the famous clinical complex known as Matrixyl 3000. Tripeptide-1 signals fibroblasts to synthesize new collagen and glycosaminoglycans. Tetrapeptide-7 acts as an anti-inflammatory peptide; it downregulates interleukin-6 (IL-6), the cellular signal responsible for destroying the skin's matrix under UV or environmental stress.

### Palmitoyl Tripeptide-38 (Matrixyl Synthe'6)
Targets six major structural components of the skin matrix: Collagen I, III, IV, Fibronectin, Hyaluronic Acid, and Laminin-5. Particularly effective at "plumping" the skin from within to smooth out deep forehead lines and crow's feet.

## Category 2: Expression-Line Smoothers (Neuro-Peptides)

Often referred to as "topical Botox," these peptides work topically to interfere with the neuromuscular signals that cause deep expression lines.

### Pathway
[Brain Signal] → SNARE Complex Flips → Acetylcholine Released → Muscle Contracts (Wrinkle)
                                                              ↑
[Acetyl Hexapeptide-8 Blocks This Complex] → Muscle Relaxes → Skin Smoothes out

### Acetyl Hexapeptide-8 (Argireline)
Mimics a crucial component of the SNARE complex. By destabilizing this complex, it limits the release of acetylcholine (the neurotransmitter that tells muscles to contract), noticeably softening dynamic lines around the eyes and forehead.

### Pentapeptide-18 (Leuphasil)
Works synergistically with Argireline but takes a different path. Mimics enkephalins (natural pain/stress modulators) to reduce calcium influx into the neuron, dampening the initial signal before it even reaches the SNARE complex.

### Dipeptide Diaminobutyroyl Benzylamide Diacetate (Syn-Ake)
A tripeptide that mimics the neuromuscular blocking mechanism found in the venom of the Temple Viper snake. Targets the nicotinic acetylcholine receptor on the muscle cell surface, keeping the muscle in a relaxed state to instantly soften expression lines.

## Category 3: Cellular Defense & Glycation Protection

### Acetylarginyltryptophyl Diphenylglycine (Relistase)
An anti-elastase peptide. As we age, overabundance of the enzyme elastase cuts up our elastin fibers, causing skin to sag and lose its "snap." This peptide inhibits elastase, restoring facial contour definition and skin resilience.

### Lactococcus Ferment Lysate
A probiotic ingredient obtained from the lysate of Lactococcus lactis. Accelerates skin barrier recovery, optimizes epidermal growth (making the outermost layers of the skin denser), and improves the quality of cellular shedding (desquamation), giving the skin a glass-like texture.

## Category 4: Direct Volumization

### Sodium Hyaluronate
The water-soluble salt form of hyaluronic acid. Holds up to 1,000x its weight in water, pulling ambient moisture into the stratum corneum. Instantly expands the intercellular space, cushioning skin, softening fine lines, and creating a smooth canvas for active peptides to penetrate.

## Master Matrix: Synergistic Actions

| Ingredient Group | Biological Target | Primary Aesthetic Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| GHK-Cu, Matrixyl 3000, Matrixyl Synthe'6 | Fibroblasts, Collagen I, III, IV & Elastin production | Firms sagging contours, thickens thinning skin, lifts deep wrinkles |
| Argireline, Leuphasil, Syn-Ake | Neuromuscular pathway & SNARE complex inhibition | Mimics Botox to smooth active expression lines (crow's feet, forehead furrows) |
| Relistase | Inhibits Elastase enzymes | Preserves skin elasticity, prevents gravity-driven sagging |
| Lactococcus Ferment Lysate | Epidermal cell differentiation & barrier proteins | Boosts barrier resilience, speeds cell renewal, refines texture |
| Sodium Hyaluronate | Stratum corneum hydration matrix | Instantly plumps fine lines, balances moisture levels |

## App Recommendation Logic
When face scan detects:
- **Aging / loss of firmness** → Recommend structural remodelers (GHK-Cu, Matrixyl 3000, Synthe'6)
- **Expression lines / dynamic wrinkles** → Recommend neuro-peptides (Argireline, Leuphasil, Syn-Ake)
- **Loss of elasticity / sagging** → Recommend Relistase + structural remodelers
- **Texture / barrier issues** → Recommend Lactococcus Ferment Lysate
- **Dehydration / fine lines** → Recommend Sodium Hyaluronate
- **Multiple concerns** → Combine across categories (they work synergistically)
