## Key Ingredients
- **Vitamin A ( cornerstone ingredient):** Retinol, retinyl palmitate, retinyl acetate
- **Vitamin C:** Ascorbyl tetraisopalmitate
- **Vitamin E:** Tocopherol
- **Antioxidants:** Green tea extract, rosemary extract
- **Peptides:** Matrixyl, Argireline
- **AHAs:** Lactic acid, glycolic acid

## Price Points
- **Entry Level:** $40-$60 (cleansers, basic moisturizers)
- **Mid Range:** $70-$120 (serums, specialized treatments)
- **High End:** $130-$180 (advanced retinol complexes, complete kits)

## Distribution Model
- **Professional Only:** Sold exclusively through authorized skin care professionals
- **Stockists:** Licensed aestheticians, medical spas, dermatology clinics
- **US Supplier:** skincare.dermaconcepts.com
- **Training Required:** Professionals must complete Environ training to stock and recommend products
- **Direct-to-Consumer:** Not available; must purchase through professional

## What Makes Them Special
1. **Vitamin STEP-UP SYSTEM™:** Graduated vitamin A system that helps skin acclimate to increasing concentrations
2. **Medical-Grade Formulations:** Developed by a plastic surgeon (Dr. Des Fernandes)
3. **Proprietary Penetration:** Uses sonophoresis (DF Mobile device) and iontophoresis for enhanced delivery
4. **Science-Backed:** Extensive clinical research on vitamin A efficacy
5. **Retinol Expertise:** Multiple forms of vitamin A in effective doses
