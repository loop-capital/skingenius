## 2. Privacy Policy (GDPR + CCPA Compliance Points)

### 2.1 Information We Collect
- **Personal Data:** Email address, name (optional), device information, usage data.
- **Sensitive Data:** Facial images uploaded for skin analysis (classified as biometric data under GDPR/CCPA).
- **Derived Data:** Skin analysis results, product recommendations, progress tracking data.

### 2.2 How We Use Your Information
- To provide and improve the SKINgenius service (e.g., analyze skin, generate recommendations).
- To communicate with you about your account, service updates, and promotional offers (if opted in).
- For internal research and development to improve our AI models (only with explicit consent for biometric data).
- To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

### 2.3 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)
- **Consent:** For processing biometric data (facial images) and sending marketing communications.
- **Contract Necessity:** To provide the core service (analysis and recommendations) based on user agreement to Terms.
- **Legitimate Interest:** For fraud prevention, security, and service improvement (balanced against user rights).
- **Legal Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations (e.g., data breach notifications).

### 2.4 Data Sharing and Third Parties
- **Service Providers:** Cloud hosting (AWS/GCP), email service, analytics tools — all under strict data processing agreements.
- **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell personal data to third parties. This is explicitly prohibited.
- **Aggregate Data:** We may share anonymized, aggregated insights with partners (e.g., "60% of users with oily skin saw improvement with niacinamide").
- **Law Enforcement:** Only if required by valid legal process (subpoena, court order).

### 2.5 User Rights and Controls
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your personal data.
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate information.
- **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** Delete your account and all associated data.
- **Right to Restriction:** Limit how we use your data.
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a portable, commonly used format.
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for biometric processing or marketing at any time.

### 2.6 Data Retention
- **Account Data:** Retained until account deletion or as required by law (e.g., tax records).
- **Facial Images:** Deleted immediately after processing unless user opts in to retain for progress tracking. If retained, stored for maximum 24 months with annual renewal consent.
- **Analysis Results:** Retained with the account unless deleted by user.
- **Backup Data:** Stored for disaster recovery, deleted after 90 days.

### 2.7 Data Security Measures
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
- Regular security audits and penetration testing.
- Access controls and employee training on data handling.
- Incident response plan for data breaches.

### 2.8 Children's Privacy
- Service not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
- If we become aware of accidental collection, we will delete the data promptly.

### 2.9 International Data Transfers
- Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries.
- Transfers protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent adequacy decisions.

### 2.10 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Specifics
- **Right to Know:** What personal information we collect, use, share, or sell.
- **Right to Delete:** Personal information we have collected (with exceptions).
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Of the sale of personal information (we do not sell, so this is moot but honored).
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** For exercising CCPA rights.
- **Do Not Sell My Info:** Link provided in footer; we honor this request despite not selling data.
- **Financial Incentive Notice:** None offered; if introduced, will comply with CCPA regulations.

### 2.11 Updates to Privacy Policy
- We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with a revised effective date.
- Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notice.

### 2.12 Contact Information
- For privacy questions or requests, contact privacy@skingenius.com.
- To exercise your rights under GDPR/CCPA, use the data subject request form in-app or email above.

## 4. Data Handling Procedures

### 4.1 Data Collection
- **User Initiation:** All personal data collection requires explicit user action (sign-up, image upload, form submission).
- **Minimization:** Only collect data necessary for the stated purpose.
- **Transparency:** Clear notice at point of collection about what data is collected and why.

### 4.2 Data Storage
- **Primary Storage:** Encrypted databases in compliant cloud regions (e.g., AWS us-east-1 with HIPAA eligibility).
- **Backups:** Encrypted, geographically redundant, with access logs.
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access; least privilege principle; MFA for all admins.

### 4.3 Data Processing
- **Image Processing:** Facial images are processed in-memory for analysis; raw images not stored unless user opts in for progress tracking.
- **Analysis Storage:** Results stored as structured data (skin scores, recommendations) linked to user ID.
- **Aggregation:** Data for improving models is aggregated and anonymized before use.

### 4.4 Data Transmission
- All APIs use HTTPS with certificate pinning where feasible.
- Internal service communication uses mTLS or equivalent mutual authentication.
- Third-party integrations only via vetted APIs with data protection clauses.

### 4.5 Data Deletion
- **User-Initiated:** Immediate deletion request processed within 24 hours; confirmation email sent.
- **Automatic:** Data deleted per retention schedules; logs maintained for compliance.
- **Upon Account Closure:** All user data marked for deletion; backups purged per schedule.

### 4.6 Incident Response
- **Detection:** Automated monitoring for unauthorized access or exfiltration.
- **Containment:** Isolate affected systems; preserve evidence.
- **Notification:** Notify affected users and regulators within required timeframes (72 hours for GDPR).
- **Remediation:** Fix vulnerabilities; notify users of steps taken.
- **Post-Incident:** Review and update security measures; provide user support.

## 5. Photo Storage and Retention Policy

### 5.1 Purpose Limitation
Facial images are collected solely for:
- Generating skin analysis and personalized recommendations
- (If opted in) Enabling progress tracking via longitudinal comparison

### 5.2 Storage Practices
- **Raw Images:** If user opts in for progress tracking, stored encrypted in object storage (e.g., AWS S3 with SSE-KMS).
- **Temporary Processing:** Images held in memory during analysis (<5 seconds), then immediately deleted from processing environment unless retention consented.
- **Metadata:** Analysis results (skin scores, regions of interest) stored separately as structured data.

### 5.3 Retention Periods
- **Without Progress Tracking Opt-In:** Images deleted immediately after analysis completes (within 10 seconds of upload).
- **With Progress Tracking Opt-In:** Images retained for maximum 24 months from upload date.
  - Users receive renewal prompts at 6, 12, and 18 months.
  - After 24 hours of non-response to renewal request, images are queued for deletion.
- **Legal Holds:** Images may be retained longer if subject to legal hold (e.g., pending litigation).

### 5.4 Deletion Process
- **User Request:** Immediate queuing for secure deletion; verification email sent upon completion.
- **Automatic Expiry:** Scheduled job runs nightly to delete images past retention limit or expired consent.
- **Method:** Cryptographic erasure or overwriting per cloud provider standards; verification of deletion.

### 5.5 User Controls
- **Opt-In/Opt-Out:** Clear toggle during upload: "Save this photo for progress tracking?" (default: OFF).
- **View Saved Images:** Users can view their stored progression images in-app.
- **Delete Individual Images:** Ability to delete specific uploaded images from their history.
- **Bulk Delete:** Option to delete all stored images and reset progress tracking.

### 5.6 Transparency
- **Upload Screen:** Clear notice: "Your photo is processed in real-time. It is not stored unless you choose to save it for progress tracking."
- **Privacy Policy:** Detailed section on image handling (as above).
- **In-App Settings:** Dedicated section for photo management with storage usage stats.

### 5.7 Special Considerations
- **Minors:** If age <13 is detected or suspected, images are immediately deleted and account flagged for review.
- **Sensitive Locations:** Service may be restricted in jurisdictions with stricter biometric laws (e.g., Illinois BIPA) — users notified if unavailable.
- **Third-Party Audits:** Annual independent audit of photo handling practices; results made available to users upon request.
