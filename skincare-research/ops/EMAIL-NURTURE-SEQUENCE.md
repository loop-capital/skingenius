## Email 2: Skin Analysis Prep Guide
**Send:** Day 2 after signup

### Subject Line
> 5 tips for the most accurate skin analysis

### Subject Line Alternatives
- How to get the best results from your first scan
- Prep your skin: a 5-step checklist

### Preview Text
> Follow these simple steps before your first SKINgenius analysis.

### Body Copy

---

Hi [First Name],

Your beta invite is coming soon — and we want you to get the most accurate results possible. Here's how to prep:

**1. Cleanse first**
Remove makeup, sunscreen, and skincare products. The AI needs to see your bare skin.

**2. Use natural light**
Stand facing a window during daylight. Avoid artificial or overhead lighting that casts shadows.

**3. Hold the camera at arm's length**
Frame your face from forehead to chin. No filters, no editing.

**4. Keep a neutral expression**
Relax your face. Don't smile or scrunch — the AI maps your skin surface and needs a clear view.

**5. Do it twice**
Take one photo in the morning and one at night. Skin changes throughout the day, and comparing both gives richer data.

**Pro tip:** Save your "before" photo. You'll want it when you see your progress 30 days in.

## Sequence Notes

- **Personalization:** Use first name if captured; fallback to "there" or "friend"
- **Send time:** 10 AM recipient's local timezone for max open rates
- **Deliverability:** Use dedicated sending domain, SPF/DKIM/DMARC configured
- **Unsubscribe:** One-click unsubscribe in footer of every email
- **Re-engagement:** If no opens after Email 3, trigger a "Still interested?" email at day 10
- **Metrics to track:** Open rate, click-through rate, CTA conversion, unsubscribe rate per email
