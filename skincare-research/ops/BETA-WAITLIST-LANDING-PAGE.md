## Value Proposition

SKINgenius combines computer vision and dermatological science to:
- Analyze your skin from a simple selfie
- Identify concerns (acne, dryness, sensitivity, aging, hyperpigmentation)
- Match you with products that work for *your* skin — not someone else's
- Track progress over time with follow-up scans
- Adjust your routine as your skin changes

**The promise:** Personalized skincare without the dermatologist price tag.

## FAQ Section (10 Questions)

### 1. How does the AI skin analysis work?
You upload a clear selfie in natural light. Our AI model, trained on dermatological datasets, identifies skin concerns like acne, dryness, sensitivity, and signs of aging. It then scores each area and generates a personalized report.

### 2. Is my photo data secure?
Absolutely. Photos are encrypted in transit and at rest. We use HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure. Your images are never sold, shared with third parties, or used to train models without explicit consent.

### 3. Do I need to pay for the beta?
No — beta access is completely free. Early members who provide feedback may also qualify for discounted premium plans after launch.

### 4. What skin types and tones does it work for?
SKINgenius is trained on diverse skin types (oily, dry, combination, sensitive) and Fitzpatrick skin tones I–VI. We actively work to reduce algorithmic bias in skincare AI.

### 5. Can I use this instead of seeing a dermatologist?
No. SKINgenius is a wellness and education tool, not a medical diagnosis. For persistent or severe skin conditions, always consult a licensed dermatologist.

### 6. What do I need to get started?
Just a smartphone with a camera and natural lighting. The analysis takes under 60 seconds. No special equipment or appointments needed.

### 7. How is my routine personalized?
We combine your skin analysis results with your goals (e.g., reduce acne, anti-aging, hydration), lifestyle factors, and product preferences (vegan, fragrance-free, budget) to rank recommendations.

### 8. What happens after the beta?
Beta users can continue with a free tier or upgrade to premium. Premium includes advanced tracking, ingredient deep-dives, and priority support.

### 9. Is this available worldwide?
Beta is launching in the US and Canada first. Join the waitlist to be notified when we expand to your region.

### 10. How can I give feedback during beta?
Beta members get direct access to our product team via in-app chat and periodic surveys. Your feedback directly shapes the product.
