- **Show the gap:** Competitors are youth-focused or clinical-only — no one serves adults at home
- **Be realistic:** We're pre-launch, but research + strategy is solid — need capital to build

**"Why would people pay when competitors are free?"**
- Depth of analysis, evidence-based recs, progress tracking, expert content — free tiers are shallow
- Gen X specifically values quality over price, willing to pay for trust

**"Can you compete with L'Oréal/Neutrogena budgets?"**
- We're not competing for mass market — premium niche with higher LTV
- Their youth-focus is our opportunity

**"What about regulatory risk?"**
- Conservative positioning as "guide" not "diagnosis"
- Clear disclaimers, advisory board oversight, HIPAA-aware design
- No medical claims — cosmetic + educational only

**"How defensible is this?"**
- Content moat: 100+ articles, clinical relationships
- Data flywheel: user photos improve AI
- Trust moat: transparency, no paid influence
- Age focus: no one else building for this demo
