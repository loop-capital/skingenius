# SKINgenius Content Strategy - Gen X Focus

## Content Pillars

### 1. Science-First Education
- Ingredient deep-dives (retinol, peptides, antioxidants)
- "How it works" explainers with clinical backing
- Before/after galleries with realistic timelines
- Myth-busting common skincare misconceptions

### 2. Age-Appropriate Inspiration
- "Better at 50 than 30" messaging
- Real women, real results (not models)
- Life-stage specific concerns (perimenopause, menopause)
- Confidence-focused, not youth-obsessed

### 3. Holistic Wellness Integration
- Sleep and skin health
- Nutrition for collagen support
- Stress management and breakouts
- Exercise and circulation

### 4. Practical Guidance
- Building a routine that works
- How to read ingredient labels
- When to see a dermatologist
- Investment vs. expense framing

## Channel Strategy

### Primary Channels
1. **Email Newsletter** — Weekly skin health education
2. **Blog/Long-form Content** — SEO-driven deep dives
3. **YouTube** — Tutorial and explainer videos
4. **Podcast** — Conversations with dermatologists, nutritionists

### Secondary Channels
5. **Instagram** — Before/after, tips, community
6. **Facebook** — Community building, live Q&As
7. **Pinterest** — Routine infographics, ingredient guides

### Avoid
- TikTok as primary channel
- Influencer-heavy strategies
- Trend-chasing content
- Youth-focused messaging

## Content Calendar Framework

### Monthly Themes (Example)
- January: New Year Skin Reset
- February: Love Your Skin (self-care)
- March: Spring Transition Routine
- April: Ingredient Spotlight (Niacinamide)
- May: Sun Protection Deep Dive
- June: Summer Skin Health
- July: Mid-Year Routine Check-in
- August: Back-to-Basics Simplification
- September: Fall Repair & Recovery
- October: Hormonal Skin Changes
- November: Gratitude & Consistency
- December: Year in Review, Planning Ahead

## Tone & Voice Guidelines

- **Expert but accessible** — No dumbing down, no jargon
- **Respectful** — Acknowledge intelligence and experience
- **Empowering** — Education over sales
- **Realistic** — Manage expectations, celebrate progress
- **Warm** — Like advice from a knowledgeable friend

## Success Metrics

- Email open rates (target: 25%+)
- Time on page for blog content (target: 4+ minutes)
- Repeat purchase rate (target: 40%+)
- Customer lifetime value (target: 2x industry average)
- Net Promoter Score (target: 50+)
