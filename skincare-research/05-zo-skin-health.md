## Key Ingredients
- **Retinol:** Multiple strengths (0.25%, 0.5%, 1%)
- **Growth Factors:** Plant-derived and synthetic
- **Vitamin C:** L-ascorbic acid and derivatives
- **Salicylic Acid:** Acne treatment
- **Benzoyl Peroxide:** Acne treatment
- **Hydroquinone:** Pigmentation (4% prescription)
- **Antioxidants:** Vitamins C, E, A
- **Enzymes:** Papain, bromelain
- **Ceramides:** Barrier repair
- **Peptides:** Anti-aging
- **Zinc Oxide:** Mineral sunscreen
