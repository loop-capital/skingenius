## The Science: Professional Peels

### What Makes PCA Peels Unique
- **First company to modify and enhance Jessner's peels**
- **10% TCA** (Trichloroacetic Acid) for superficial and medium-depth peeling
- **20% Lactic Acid** as part of natural moisturizing factor
- **Customized formulations** for different skin types
- **Professional-only application** for most peels
- **Blend of acids + targeted active ingredients** addressing cause of each concern

### Types of PCA Professional Peels

#### 1. Sensi Peel®
- **For:** All skin types, including sensitive skin
- **Strength:** Universal 6% TCA and lactic acid solution
- **Benefits:** Skin smoothing, strengthening, hydration, melanogenesis inhibition
- **Great for:** First-time peel clients, sensitive skin

#### 2. Ultra Peel®
- **For:** More resilient skin types
- **Strength:** Medium-depth
- **Benefits:** Addresses fine lines, wrinkles, pigmentation
- **Great for:** Mature skin, sun damage

#### 3. PCA Peel® (Modified Jessner's)
- **For:** Oily skin, acne-prone, surface lines
- **Benefits:** Softens appearance of surface lines around eyes and nasolabial folds, improves acne, smooths texture, evens skin tone
- **Great for:** Oily skin, acne, sun damage

#### 4. PCA Peel® with Hydroquinone
- **For:** Even skin tone (no sensitivity to hydroquinone)
- **Benefits:** Quickly lightens pigment discolorations (melasma, actinic damage), inhibits acne, corrects post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation
- **Great for:** Hyperpigmentation, melasma

#### 5. PCA Peel® with Hydroquinone & Resorcinol
- **For:** More intensive pigment correction
- **Benefits:** Deeper pigment treatment
- **Great for:** Stubborn hyperpigmentation

#### 6. Retexturize: Therapeutic Pumpkin Peel
- **For:** Gentle exfoliation
- **Active:** Pumpkin enzymes
- **Great for:** All skin types, including sensitive

#### 7. Revitalize: Therapeutic Papaya Enzyme Peel
- **For:** Gentle enzyme-based exfoliation
- **Active:** Papaya enzymes
- **Great for:** Sensitive skin

#### 8. Clarify: Therapeutic Salicylic Acid Peel
- **For:** Acne-prone skin
- **Active:** Salicylic acid
- **Great for:** Acne, oily skin

#### 9. 4% Pure Retinol Peel
- **For:** Advanced anti-aging
- **Strength:** 4% retinol
- **Great for:** Significant aging concerns

#### 10. 6% Pure Retinol Peel
- **For:** Maximum strength anti-aging
- **Strength:** 6% retinol
- **Great for:** Advanced aging, professional-only

## The Peel Hierarchy
PCA offers a complete range:
- **Entry:** Enzyme peels (Pumpkin, Papaya) - no downtime
- **Gentle:** Sensi Peel - minimal downtime, all skin types
- **Medium:** Ultra Peel, PCA Peel - moderate downtime
- **Intensive:** PCA with Hydroquinone - significant results
- **Maximum:** 6% Retinol Peel - professional only, maximum correction

This allows professionals to customize treatments based on client skin type, concern, and downtime tolerance.
