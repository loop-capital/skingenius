## The Science: OS-01 Peptide

### What Is It?
- **OS-01** is a proprietary decapeptide-52 (10-amino-acid peptide)
- **First ingredient scientifically proven to reverse skin's biological age**
- Developed through bioinformatics and genomics research
- **900+ peptides tested** over 5+ years of R&D
- Targets cellular senescence (one of the key drivers of skin aging)

### How It Works
1. **Reduces Senescent Cells:** Decreases accumulation of aged "zombie" cells in skin
2. **Prevents Domino Effect:** Stops damaged cells from releasing inflammatory factors that turn neighboring healthy cells senescent
3. **Collagen & Elastin:** Signals skin cells to trigger collagen and hyaluronic acid production
4. **Cellular Repair:** Aids in cellular repair processes
5. **Barrier Strengthening:** Helps strengthen the skin barrier

### Clinical Validation
- **Published Research:** Zonari, A., et al. "Senotherapeutic peptide treatment reduces biological age and senescence burden in human skin models." npj Aging, 9(1), 1-15. 2023.
- **Peer-reviewed data** backing claims
- **Clinical studies** on efficacy
